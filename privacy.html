Privacy Policy for HideM

Last Updated: November 30, 2025

HideM is a secure media vault designed to protect personal photos, videos, notes, and related files. This Privacy Policy explains how HideM handles information, including how third-party services such as Google AdMob and Google Play Services may process certain technical data. This policy applies to all current and future features of HideM, including local vault storage, media management, notes, security features, and any additional utilities introduced later.

1. Information We Do NOT Collect

HideM does not collect, store, share, or transmit the following information:

  • Personal identity information (name, email, phone number)
  • Your photos, videos, files, folders, or notes
  • Contacts, messages, call logs
  • Precise location or GPS data
  • Passwords, biometric data, or authentication tokens
  • Analytics data (we do not track user behavior)
  • Device identifiers collected by us
  • Cloud backups or server-stored data

All private media stays 100% offline on your device.

2. Local Data Stored on Your Device

HideM stores certain data locally on your device to provide the app’s core functionality. This includes:

  • Encrypted photos and videos you import into the vault
  • Private notes or text-based content
  • Folders you create inside the app
  • Your chosen PIN or Pattern lock (securely stored)
  • App preferences such as theme, layout, or settings

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible by us.

3. Device Storage & Real-Time Storage Info

HideM displays:

  • Used storage
  • Available storage

This information is read directly from your device and is not collected, stored, or transmitted. It remains entirely on your device.

4. Permissions We Request

HideM may request specific permissions required for functionality:

4.1 Storage / Media Access

  • Used only to allow you to choose and import files into the secure vault.
  • We cannot access files unless you manually select them.

4.2 Network Access

  • Used only to load advertisements from Google AdMob.
  • HideM never uses the internet to upload or transmit your private media.

HideM does not request any unnecessary permissions.

5. Advertising (AdMob)

HideM uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may automatically collect device and technical information necessary for delivering ads. This may include:

  • IP address
  • Device type, OS version, and model
  • Approximate (non-GPS) location
  • Advertising ID
  • App interaction information (for ad performance)
  • Cookies or mobile identifiers

This information is collected by AdMob, not by HideM. We do not access or store AdMob data.

AdMob Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

6. Personalized & Non-Personalized Ads

Depending on your region:

  • GDPR regions: You will receive a consent form (via Google's UMP SDK).
  • EEA / UK users may choose personalized or non-personalized ads.
  • CCPA users can opt-out of ad personalization.
  • Other regions receive standard AdMob ads.

HideM fully complies with global ad consent requirements.

7. Crash Reporting (Google Play Services)

If enabled by system settings, Google Play Services may collect anonymous crash logs. These logs:

  • Contain no private media
  • Contain no personal identity data
  • Are used only to diagnose app crashes

8. Children’s Privacy

HideM is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal data from children.

AdMob automatically applies child-appropriate ad filtering as required by Google Play policies.

9. User Rights (GDPR / CCPA / International Laws)

Depending on your region, you may have the right to:

  • Opt-out of ad personalization
  • Control or reset advertising ID
  • Access information collected by Google AdMob
  • Withdraw consent in applicable regions

Users can manage Google ad preferences here: https://www.google.com/settings/ads

10. Data Deletion

When you uninstall HideM, the following are permanently deleted:

  • Encrypted photos, videos, and media stored in the vault
  • All private notes
  • Security lock data (PIN/Pattern)
  • App preferences and settings

We do not store any copies or backups.

AdMob-related data must be deleted through Google’s privacy settings.

13. PIN & Pattern Recovery Instructions

HideM provides a secure offline recovery method if you forget your PIN or Pattern. This recovery process is handled entirely on your device and no information is sent to any server.

13.1 PIN Reset Process

  • To trigger the PIN reset mechanism, you must press the Backspace/Delete button exactly 9 times.
  • Important: While pressing Backspace 9 times, you must not enter any digits.
  • After the 9 backspace presses, enter the number 3.

If done correctly, you will be prompted to create a new PIN.

13.2 Pattern Reset Process

To reset your pattern, you must draw a specific sequence on the pattern grid in the following order:

1 → 5 → 9 → 8 → 4 → 7 → 2 → 6 → 3

When this sequence is completed correctly, the app will allow you to set a new pattern.

This reset feature works only on your device and does not share any data with us or any third party.
